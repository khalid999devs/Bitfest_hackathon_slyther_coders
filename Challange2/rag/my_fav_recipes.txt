Name: Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:
1. Chicken Breast:
   - Quantity: 300g
   - Description: Boneless and skinless chicken breasts work best for this dish. Dice or slice into thin strips before cooking for faster preparation.
2. Fettuccine:
   - Quantity: 200g
   - Description: Classic pasta for Alfredo sauce. Cook al dente for the best texture.
3. Heavy Cream:
   - Quantity: 1 cup
   - Description: Use fresh, full-fat cream to achieve a rich and creamy sauce.
4. Parmesan Cheese:
   - Quantity: 100g
   - Description: Finely grated Parmesan cheese adds a sharp, nutty flavor. Avoid pre-shredded cheese for the best melting results.
5. Garlic:
   - Quantity: 2 cloves
   - Description: Minced or finely chopped to infuse the sauce with aromatic flavor.
6. Butter:
   - Quantity: 2 tbsp
   - Description: Unsalted butter is preferred to control the saltiness of the dish.

Taste:
- Savory: This dish combines the creamy, buttery richness of the sauce with the savory notes of Parmesan and garlic.

Cuisine Type:
- Italian: A classic Italian pasta dish that originated in Rome and has become a global favorite.

Preparation Time:
- 30 Minutes: This includes cooking the chicken, boiling the pasta, and preparing the Alfredo sauce.

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Chicken:
   - Heat a skillet over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of butter.
   - Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.
   - Cook the chicken pieces until golden brown on both sides and fully cooked (internal temperature of 165°F or 74°C). Remove from the skillet and set aside.

2. Cook the Fettuccine:
   - Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
   - Add the fettuccine and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
   - Drain the pasta, reserving ½ cup of pasta water, and set aside.

3. Make the Alfredo Sauce:
   - In the same skillet used for the chicken, melt the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
   - Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
   - Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer, stirring continuously.
   - Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Adjust the consistency with the reserved pasta water if needed.

4. Combine:
   - Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, coating it with the Alfredo sauce.
   - Add the drained fettuccine and toss until everything is evenly coated with the sauce.

5. Serve:
   - Transfer the pasta to serving plates or a large serving dish.
   - Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and freshly chopped parsley, if desired.
   - Serve hot and enjoy!

Additional Tips:
- For extra flavor, consider adding a pinch of nutmeg to the Alfredo sauce.
- Pair this dish with a side of garlic bread and a crisp green salad for a complete meal.
- Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat gently on the stove with a splash of cream to restore the sauce's creaminess.
